Patient Registration

Patients can register in two ways:

1. If your patient data already exists in the system, enter your Patient ID.
2. If you are a new patient and do not yet have a Patient ID, leave it blank and the system will create one for you automatically.







Password Rules:
- Minimum 8 characters
- Maximum 15 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one special character



Enter Patient ID only if your patient record already exists in the system. If you are a new patient, leave it blank and a new patient record with a new Patient ID will be created automatically.
